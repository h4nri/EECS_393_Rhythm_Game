Determining distance (y) between beats:
1) Find BPM and convert it to BPS
2) Divide BPS by fall speed of notes
3) Divide 1 by the above quotient

1920x1080 images should have position.y = 5.6, rotation.x = -65, and scale.x and scale.y both = 1.3
